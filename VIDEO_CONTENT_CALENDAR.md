# Church's Cookout — 90-Day Video Content Calendar

## How to Use This
- **Reels/TikTok:** Film all pieces for 2 weeks in one 3-4 hour batch session
- **YouTube:** Film one long-form video per week during the same batch session
- Post Reels and TikTok simultaneously (same video, re-captioned for each platform)
- Captions should always include: relevant hashtags + "Full recipe at churchscookout.com"

## Video Production Template

### Short-Form (Reels / TikTok)

```
FRAME 1 (0–2 sec): Hero shot of finished dish. No title card needed.
VOICEOVER: [Hook statement — one sentence, bold claim]

FRAME 2–END (3–50 sec): 
- Ingredients flat lay (2 sec)
- Key technique (5–10 sec)
- Cook process fast-cut (20–30 sec)
- Final plating (5 sec)

LAST FRAME: Dish name + "Full recipe → churchscookout.com"
AUDIO: Trending sound or upbeat background music
```

### Long-Form (YouTube)

```
0:00–0:30  — Hook: Show the dish. "This is [dish]. Here's how I make it, why it tastes the way it does, and the one technique most people miss."
0:30–2:00  — Story/Context: Where this dish comes from, your connection to it
2:00–10:00 — Full recipe: Ingredients, technique, step by step
10:00–12:00 — Plating + taste test
12:00–13:00 — Wrap: "If you want the full recipe, it's on churchscookout.com. The meal planner on the site lets you add this to your week automatically."
13:00–end  — Subscribe CTA
```
